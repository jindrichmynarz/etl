The {{page.title}} component provides a form to fill in DCAT-AP v1.1 Dataset metadata. The typical usage is in each data preparation pipeline to store and produce dataset related metadata. Its output can be used by the DCAT-AP Distribution component to reuse the Dastaset IRI, languages and issued and modified dates, and also by the DCAT-AP to CKAN component for loading the metadata into CKAN.
